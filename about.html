About the workshop

Put good material back to work.

Where old stuff gets a new life and earns its keep.

The approach

Start with what’s already here.

Chip is the maker behind Workshop Revival in Long Beach, California. He looks for useful potential in would-be trash, then repairs, repurposes, and revives everyday materials.

The goal is straightforward: practical builds that look good, work hard, and cost little.
